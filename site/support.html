Recipient Guard — Support

Help with the Outlook add-in that warns you before sending to a likely wrong recipient.

Contact

Email support@recipientguard.co.uk and we'll get back to you. Please include your Outlook version (new Outlook on Windows, Outlook on the web, or classic Outlook) and a description of the issue.

Common questions

  • How does it decide a recipient is wrong? It flags recipients outside your organisation, recipients that share a name or username with another recipient, and — with Smart detection on — recipients that differ from the address you usually use for that person.
  • Smart detection isn't finding my contacts. Click Turn on smart detection and accept the one-time permission prompt. It needs a Microsoft 365 account and works on new Outlook and Outlook on the web.
  • It flagged a recipient that was correct. Choose Send anyway, or use Don't warn about this address to stop flagging it in future.
  • How do I remove it? Remove the add-in from Outlook's add-in management. To revoke its permissions, visit myapps.microsoft.com.
